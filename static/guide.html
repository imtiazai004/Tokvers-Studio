TikTok Integration — What's Happening
Important: TikTok's Content Posting API requires developer approval from TikTok before any app can post videos on behalf of users. This approval is being applied for — it can take 1–4 weeks. Until then, videos are generated and saved on your computer, and you upload them to TikTok manually.

📋 TikTok API Approval Timeline

  • ✓
    TikTok Developer Account Created

    Developer app registered at developer.tiktok.com. One app handles all clients.

  • ⏳
    Content Posting API — Approval Pending

    Application submitted to TikTok for Content Posting API access. TikTok reviews manually and approves within 1–4 weeks. No action needed from you.

  • →
    Once Approved: Connect Your TikTok Account

    A "Connect TikTok" button will appear in the dashboard. You click it, log into your TikTok account, and grant permission. The system then posts directly to your account.

  • →
    Auto-Posting + Scheduling Goes Live

    Generate a video → it automatically posts to your TikTok at the best time. Schedule weeks of content in advance.

For Now: How to Post Your Generated Videos

Videos are saved in the output/ folder on your computer. Until TikTok API is approved:

  1. Generate your video from the dashboard.
  2. Video saves to the output/ folder — open it and find the .mp4 file.
  3. Open TikTok on your phone or tiktok.com/upload on desktop.
  4. Upload the video, add your caption and hashtags (the system suggests these), and post.
Quick Start — Get Your First Video in 10 Minutes
  1. Go to API Settings and enter your Anthropic and ElevenLabs keys. These two are enough to start.
  2. Add either a Grok or Google AI Studio key for video generation.
  3. Go back to the Dashboard.
  4. Enter your product name, niche, and product URL → click Generate Video.
  5. Wait 2–5 minutes. Your video will appear ready to download.
